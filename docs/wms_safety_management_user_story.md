# Safety Management (wms_safety_management) User Story

## User Story
As a warehouse safety manager, I want to track and manage safety incidents, training, and compliance so that I can maintain a safe working environment and meet regulatory requirements.

## Acceptance Criteria
- System tracks safety incidents with detailed reporting and analysis
- System manages safety training and certification for all employees
- System handles safety inspections and audit management
- System tracks personal protective equipment (PPE) usage and compliance
- System monitors safety compliance across all warehouse operations
- System performs risk assessment and mitigation planning
- System measures and tracks safety performance metrics
- System generates safety analytics and compliance dashboards
- System sends alerts for safety violations or potential risks
- System maintains safety documentation for audit purposes

## Business Value
This feature will improve workplace safety by tracking incidents and ensuring compliance, reduce accidents through proactive risk management, maintain regulatory compliance, and provide visibility into safety performance for continuous improvement.
