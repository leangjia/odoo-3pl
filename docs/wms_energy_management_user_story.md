# Energy Management (wms_energy_management) User Story

## User Story
As a warehouse operations manager, I want to track and optimize energy consumption in the warehouse so that I can reduce operating costs and meet sustainability goals.

## Acceptance Criteria
- System monitors and tracks energy consumption across warehouse equipment and systems
- System analyzes equipment energy usage patterns and provides insights
- System calculates carbon footprint based on energy consumption data
- System provides energy cost allocation and reporting by department/operation
- System manages peak demand monitoring and optimization strategies
- System supports energy efficiency optimization recommendations
- System integrates with renewable energy sources when available
- System generates energy analytics and sustainability dashboards
- System provides alerts for unusual energy consumption patterns
- System tracks energy KPIs against sustainability targets

## Business Value
This feature will reduce operational costs through energy optimization, support corporate sustainability goals, provide visibility into energy-related expenses, and enable data-driven decisions for energy management improvements.
