# Quality Control (wms_quality_control) User Story

## User Story
As a warehouse quality manager, I want to implement quality inspection, testing, and compliance tracking so that I can ensure product quality and meet customer requirements.

## Acceptance Criteria
- System supports quality inspection processes at various warehouse stages
- System manages quality testing procedures and results tracking
- System tracks compliance with quality standards and regulations
- System handles quality hold and release processes for non-conforming products
- System provides quality reporting and analytics for continuous improvement
- System integrates quality data with inventory management for traceability
- System supports configurable quality checklists and procedures
- System manages quality certificates and documentation requirements
- System tracks quality metrics and KPIs for performance monitoring
- System provides alerts for quality issues and non-compliance events

## Business Value
This feature will ensure product quality standards are maintained throughout warehouse operations, reduce quality-related customer issues, provide traceability for quality events, and support compliance with industry quality standards and regulations.
